What Keynetics Measures

Two Metrics.
Every Session.

The Keynetics sensor captures the two data streams that matter most for movement quality, injury prevention, and performance optimization — and does it in real time, on the gym floor.

1

Joint Angles & Range of Motion

Quantifies how far and how accurately joints move through prescribed patterns. Every rep. Every session. Over time, the data reveals improvement that eyes alone cannot detect.

2

Balance & Stability

Measures postural control and weight distribution. Critical for injury prevention benchmarking, rehabilitation tracking, and athletic performance optimization.
