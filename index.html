// Our metrics
Five proprietary measurements.

The signals other systems miss.

001 · Joint Mechanics
JAD
Joint Angular Deviation
Quantifies how far each joint deviates from its symmetric counterpart through a movement cycle. Rewards quality, not just quantity.
002 · Force
BFSI
Bilateral Force Symmetry Index
Measures left-right load distribution under dynamic effort. The earliest indicator of compensation, fatigue, and re-injury risk.
003 · Movement
SIS
Stride Index Score
A composite of contact time, symmetry, and angular consistency, normalized across athletes for population-level comparison.

// How it works
Three steps. No lab required.

From sensor on to clinical-grade insight in under five minutes.

01

Capture

Place the KEYSense sensor pack on the athlete using the quick-clip strap system. Record a single movement — squat, sprint, jump, gait — directly from the body. 125 Hz of 6-axis inertial data streams in real time. No suit. No cables. No lab.

≈ 90 seconds
02

Model

Our biomechanics engine fuses sensor signals into a full kinematic chain, computes all five Keynetics metrics, and benchmarks against validated population norms — processed on the sensor edge and encrypted end-to-end.

≈ 60 seconds
03

Act

You get a defensible, exportable report. The clinician can prescribe. The coach can adjust. The athlete can see themselves move — with data they can trust and share.

≈ instant
