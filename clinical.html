Clinical Workflow

Fits Into Your
Existing Practice.

Keynetics is not designed to replace existing clinical documentation systems. It adds an objective movement data layer to your existing session and outcome documentation workflow.

Positioning Note: The Keynetics clinical module is designed as a functional outcome tracking tool — not a diagnostic instrument. This positioning keeps the platform outside the scope of FDA device regulation and HIPAA-adjacent documentation requirements while still delivering clinically useful movement data. Legal review is completed prior to clinical market launch.
1

Client Intake
